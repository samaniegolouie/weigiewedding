Our Wedding

Our Review in the Zion Event Place

From the very start of planning our wedding, Zion Event Place gave us their full support. They never left us hanging, and their teamwork was amazing — they helped us bring all our ideas to life and even shared their own suggestions to make our event even better.

They always responded quickly, and we never once felt neglected. The collaboration between their team and ours was so smooth, even down to details like the rooms and food — their service was top-notch. What we loved most as a couple was that they offered custom packages and installment fees, so it wasn’t too heavy on the budget for couples getting married. Even though we had a custom setup, their service remained premium all throughout.

Even with our outside suppliers, they were there to help and coordinate, working hand-in-hand even if it wasn’t their supplier. They’re not just an event place — they truly partner with you from start to finish.

Thank you so much to Zion Event Place for handling and taking care of us. 5 stars from us! Highly recommended not just for weddings, but for any event. For our wedding, they were simply the best!

Our Review in the Bobby Arts Statue

Bobby Arts Statue was such a wonderful idea, especially as a gift for our parents. They captured the faces so perfectly — even though our parent had already passed away, the artwork made it feel like they were still with us. It moved our mom to tears when she saw the gift. 5 stars from us! Highly recommended, especially as a wedding gift.

Our Review in the Amazing Smile

I’m not sure if this was the first time something like this was done at a wedding, but Amazing Smile had such a brilliant idea for our special day. Our guests were a mix of young and old, and what happened was almost everyone turned into kids again because they were so entertained by all the fun activities, especially the game booths. It was also great because it kept lines from getting long at the other booths since everyone was busy enjoying different things. We thought this would just be for the kids, but even the adults absolutely loved it. I don’t know if others have done this before, but it’s truly such a great idea, especially if you have an SDE — because while waiting, everyone stays happy and engaged. Definitely 5 stars from us! Highly recommended, especially for weddings.

Our Review in the Todays Party Shop

The service from Today’s Party Shop was amazing, especially for our wedding guests. They really enjoyed the 360 booth and were so happy receiving their video recordings instantly on their phones. Everyone had so much fun with the 360 video experience that they didn’t even notice the time — even while we were doing our other video segments like our prenup and SDE for the wedding. Definitely 5 stars from us. Highly recommended, especially for weddings. If you have an SDE, this is perfect because it keeps your guests entertained.

Our Review in the Scents For Events

The package we got from Scents For Events was excellent. It included a roaming photographer, which was a brilliant idea because it avoided long lines at a typical photo booth. They went straight to our guests and handed them their photo prints right away. We also loved the free telephone booth for quick voice messages for us. As for the perfumes, everyone was thrilled—even our principal and secondary sponsors enjoyed receiving them. 5 stars for them! Highly recommended, especially for weddings. All our guests were delighted with their service. Thank you, Scents For Events, for adding so much joy to our special day!

Our Review in the Sleight Tattoo & Haircut

Sleight Tattoo & Haircut provides excellent service. Their event consultant gave thoughtful recommendations on what haircut would suit the shape of my head as a groom-to-be. On the wedding day itself, they didn’t let me down—no matter what I wore or what the wedding motif was, they made sure I looked great. 5 stars for them! Highly recommended, especially for wedding looks. Thank you for giving me a new level of confidence and a fresh look for my special day.
